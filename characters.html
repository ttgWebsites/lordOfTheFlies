Characters

  • Ralph
  • Ralph is the protagonist and the leader of one group of survivors
  • He is very wise and compassionate for his young age of 12, and he works to restore order on the island
  • Jack
  • Jack is the antagonist of the novel and leads the other group of boys
  • He is very controlling and demanding, and he instills a life of savagery on the boys in his group
  • Piggy
  • Piggy is the smartest of all the survivors
  • He is referred to as piggy due to being fat and he also has asthma
  • He is Ralph's best friend and works hard to help the group survive, however he eventually dies
  • Roger
  • Roger is a very secretive person who preys on the younger and weaker children
  • He is Jack's first follower, and second in command as he follows the evil demands of Jack
  • Simon
  • He is the only one with morals in Jack's group
  • He spends most of his time alone
  • When he goes in search of the beast he ends up being crucified
  • Sam and Eric
  • Sam and Eric are twins and do everything together
  • They initially follow Ralph, however eventually are convinced to switch to Jack's side
