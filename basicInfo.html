Basic Info

  • Title
  • Lord of the Flies
  • Author
  • William Golding
  • Published
  • England. Faber and Faber, 1954.
  • Genre
  • The novel falls into many different genres such as allegory, adventure, and loss of innocence.
  • Setting
  • The novel is set in the near future, which would be the near future of 1954, on a tropical island.
  • Point Of View
  • The novel is told from the third person point of view, mostly following the character Ralph, however sometimes following the characters Jack or Simon. The narrator seems to have a deep understanding of the inner workings of the characters minds and thoughts.
  • Conflict
  • When a group of young boys become stranded on an island following a plane crash they must find a way to survive. The boys, each having different personalities, cope with the crash differently and have different methods of surviving. Some turn to trying to follow the structures of normal society, while others fall into a lifestyle of anything goes and savagery.
